Get to Know About Techllective

Techllective is a non-profit organization founded in 2023 by eight software engineering students from Liberia who study in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus. It started as a group to help each other and other students in the universities, but later grew into a full-fledged organization with its own vision, mission, and goals.

Vision and Mission

Techllective's motto is "Building, Developing, Connecting, and Impacting for Empowerment." Its mission is to empower and inspire students to utilize their technical skills and knowledge for social impact. Its vision is to leverage technology and collective efforts to create a better world.

The achievements of an organization are the results of the combined effort of each individual.

Vince Lombardi

Goals Techllective's goals are to: Empower individuals through technology Inspire innovation and creativity Enhance productivity and teamwork Services Techllective offers a variety of services, including: Web design and development App design and development Cloud computing Quality assurance Software testing Systems integration Events and Hackathons

Techllective also hosts events and hackathons for students to learn new skills, collaborate on projects, and network with each other. Impact Techllective is a unique and innovative organization that is making a real impact in the world. It is empowering students to use their technical skills to make a difference in their communities and the world.
